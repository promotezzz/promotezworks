Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 5, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. Below is our simple privacy policy regarding promotezworks projects.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, track, or store any personal data or user telemetry. Our mods and modpacks run entirely locally on your client machine and do not send data back to us.

2. Third-Party Platforms

Our projects and file downloads are hosted publicly on Modrinth and GitHub. Any generic download counts or metrics are collected directly by those platforms under the privacy policies of Modrinth and GitHub.

3. Multiplayer Logging

When you play on a multiplayer server using our modpacks, standard log statistics (such as usernames and IP addresses) are handled exclusively by your server hosting provider. promotezworks developers have no access to these server logs.
